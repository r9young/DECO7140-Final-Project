Bean Bag Toss

Bean Bag Toss, also known as Cornhole, is a classic outdoor game that brings players of all ages together for friendly competition and fun. The game involves two raised platforms, each with a circular hole near the top. Players take turns tossing bean bags from a distance, aiming to land them on the platform or, for maximum points, through the hole.

Hopscotch

Hopscotch is a traditional playground game that has entertained children for generations. Played on a series of numbered squares drawn on the ground, it combines agility, balance, and strategic thinking.

Kite Flying

Kite flying is a timeless outdoor activity that brings joy to people of all ages. With vibrant colors and unique designs soaring high in the sky, it evokes a sense of freedom and playfulness.

Frisbee Fun

Playing frisbee is a classic way for kids and adults to come together for some outdoor fun. With just a flying disc, a group of friends, and an open space, you can enjoy an active and joyful game that encourages teamwork and physical fitness.

Spikeball

Spikeball (or Roundnet) is an exhilarating team sport that combines elements of volleyball and four square. Played on a small circular net with a ball, it requires agility, speed, and strategic thinking. Teams of two compete to spike the ball onto the net and prevent their opponents from returning it.

Croquet

Croquet is a classic outdoor game that combines skill, strategy, and patience. Played on a grass lawn with mallets, balls, and hoops (called wickets), players aim to navigate their balls through a series of wickets and hit the final stake.
